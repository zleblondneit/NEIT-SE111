Review Of Prisoners (2013)

Zachary LeBlond, January 30th 2024

★★★★★

Prisoners as a movie tackles and extremely heavy topic, it handles itself extremely well with the controversy surrounding its theme. The actors in this movie did an outstanding job playing their respective characters and showing experession. The entire movie showcases the descent of Keller Dover and they could not have chosen any more qualified than Hugh Jackson. Detective Loki goes through his own trials and tribulations and Jake Gylenhall plays this role in spades. The entire film is based on suspense and without spoiling the film the movie wraps itself up in a beautiful crescendo, correctly closing all points displayed throughout the film. Prisoners is a movie I would give anything to watch for the first time and I highly reccomend it to anyone who is into anything in the crime genre.

Trivia About Prisoners

  • Paul Dano used a form of method acting to perfect his role in the film

  • The film was first given an NC-17 rating before it was changed to an R rating

  • Ryan Gosling originally auditoned for the role of Detective Loki before it was given to Jake Gylenhall
